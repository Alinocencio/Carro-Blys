Designed with Aerodynamics and Intuitive Technology

BMW M440i convertible aerodynamics have improved. The basic Aerodynamic Kit adds a diffuser, side skirts, and larger front air intakes. BMW provides TeleServices. In some cities, Connected Package Professional features Real-Time Traffic Information and On-Street Parking Information, Hazard Preview, BMW Remote Services with Stolen Vehicle Recovery, Remote Door Unlock, and My BMW App. Navigation is possible with the Intelligent Personal Assistant voice command.

Interior

The luxurious interior of the BMW M440i is designed with performance in mind. Sport Seats: These seats are made to be comfortable and give you the best support when driving aggressively.Wrapped in premium leather and Alcantara. A lit-up M4 badge gives the car that extra sporty touch. M4 Pedals are made of stainless steel with rubber treads, and they show off the high-quality finishes that make BMW M4 cars stand out. Steering Wheels: A thick 3-spoke M4 steering wheel improves control, and an available M4 Performance steering wheel goes one step further with track features like Alcantara grips and a centering stripe.

Exterior

BMW M440i convertibles, which already have a higher profile than most of their competitors, now have even more room to move around on the ground. an alternative with a soft top that is reportedly 40% lighter. There is also a bigger 19-inch alloy wheel option. Even the stock 18-inch alloy wheels are quite grippable. The Aerodynamic package improves handling by increasing the size of the front air intake, adding a rear diffuser, and moldings to the side windows. These, together with the distinctive front bumper design, give the Convertible a distinctly athletic air. LED headlights and taillights are mounted for a more modern look.
